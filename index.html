Why ARBA

The Amazon holds the world’s most potent reservoir of plant bioactives — yet its ingredients are too often marketed as exotic curiosities rather than science-backed innovations. ARBA unites brands, scientists, NGOs, and forest communities to change that narrative — transforming the Amazon from a source of raw materials into a living model of regeneration.

Our Mission

To elevate Amazonian ingredients into global recognition through education, transparency, and regenerative sourcing, proving that beauty can protect the forest.

Our Approach

We work at the intersection of science, storytelling, and community-led regeneration — building a coalition that aligns commerce with conservation.

What We Do

From knowledge-sharing to market activation, ARBA advances a regenerative future for Amazonian ingredients.

Educate & Inspire

Build global understanding of Amazonian ingredients through open-source data, ingredient monographs, and science communication.

Define Integrity

Establish regenerative sourcing principles, verification tools, and impact metrics.

Strengthen Forest-Based Communities

Support the survival, autonomy, and recognition of forest communities, honouring their vital role in conservation and traditional knowledge transmission.

Tell Shared Stories

Co-create films, photography, and narratives that connect brands to forest communities.

Activate Markets

Drive awareness through campaigns, events, and advocacy partnerships.

Innovate Together

Support collaborative R&D, extraction technology, and circular business models.

Who Should Join

ARBA is a coalition for organisations committed to regenerative beauty, science, and Amazonian stewardship.

  • Beauty, wellness, and personal-care brands using natural ingredients
  • Ingredient manufacturers, R&D labs, and formulators
  • Amazonian cooperatives and producer networks
  • NGOs, certification bodies, and academic partners
  • Impact investors, climate-finance, and philanthropic actors
